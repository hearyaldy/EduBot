# Privacy Policy for EduBot - AI Homework Helper

**Last Updated:** September 4, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to EduBot ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our EduBot mobile application (the "App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you register for an account, we collect:
- **Email Address**: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
- **Name**: Optional display name for personalization
- **Password**: Securely encrypted for account security

### Usage Data
We automatically collect certain information when you use our App:
- **Questions Asked**: Text and image-based homework questions you submit
- **AI Responses**: Generated answers and explanations provided by our AI service
- **Usage Statistics**: Number of daily questions used, feature usage patterns
- **Session Data**: App usage duration and frequency

### Device Information
- **Device Type**: Mobile device model and operating system
- **App Version**: Version of EduBot you're using
- **Crash Reports**: Technical data to improve app stability
- **Advertising ID**: For personalized advertising (if ads are enabled)

### Camera and Storage Access
- **Camera**: Used only for scanning homework problems when you choose to use the camera feature
- **Photo Library**: Access to select images for homework questions (with your permission)
- **Local Storage**: Saved questions and app preferences stored locally on your device

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Purposes
- **AI Homework Assistance**: Process your questions through Google Gemini AI to provide educational help
- **Account Management**: Maintain your user account and preferences
- **Usage Tracking**: Monitor daily question limits based on account type
- **Service Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality

### Communication
- **Account Updates**: Password reset emails and account verification
- **Support**: Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
- **Service Announcements**: Important updates about the App (rare, essential communications only)

### Account Data
- **Active Accounts**: Retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

### Usage Data
- **Question History**: Saved questions retained until you delete them or close your account

## Children's Privacy

EduBot is designed to help students of all ages with homework. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

### For Users Under 13
- Parental consent is required for account creation
- Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information
- Limited data collection focused on educational functionality

### For Users 13-17
- May create accounts with parental awareness
- Parents can contact us to access or delete their minor child's data

## Specific Feature Privacy Details

### AI Question Processing
- **Local Processing**: Initial image processing may occur on your device
- **Cloud Processing**: Questions are sent to Google Gemini for AI responses
- **No Human Review**: Your questions are processed automatically without human intervention
- **Temporary Processing**: Questions are not permanently stored by AI services beyond processing needs

### Voice Input (If Enabled)
- **Device Processing**: Speech-to-text conversion occurs on your device when possible
- **No Voice Storage**: Voice recordings are not stored; only converted text is processed
- **Permission Based**: Voice features require explicit permission

### Saved Questions
- **User Control**: You choose which questions to save
- **Local and Cloud**: Saved questions stored both locally and in secure cloud storage
- **Export Option**: You can export all saved questions at any time
- **Deletion**: Permanently delete individual or all saved questions

### Premium Features
- **Payment Processing**: Payment information handled by app store (Apple App Store/Google Play Store)
- **No Financial Data**: We do not store credit card or payment information
- **Subscription Management**: Managed through your app store account

## Account Types and Data Collection

### Guest Users (No Account)
- **Limited Data**: Only usage statistics and temporary session data
- **No Personal Info**: No email, name, or persistent data collection
- **Local Storage Only**: Questions saved locally on device only
- **Daily Limits**: 30 questions per day, tracked locally

### Registered Users
- **Account Data**: Email, optional name, encrypted password
- **Cloud Sync**: Questions and preferences synced securely
- **Increased Limits**: 60 questions per day
- **Account Management**: Full profile and data control

### Premium Users
- **Same as Registered**: Plus premium feature access
- **No Ads**: Ad-free experience, no advertising data collection
- **Unlimited Questions**: No daily usage limits
- **Priority Support**: Enhanced customer service
